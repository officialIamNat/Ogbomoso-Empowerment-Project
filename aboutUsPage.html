Welcome To Ogbomoso

Building the
Ogbomoso of Tomorrow

This empowerment scheme is a pioneering effort to turn Ogbomoso into a beacon of entrepreneurship, learning, and cultural revival. It is a clarion call to harness the power of skill, innovation, and collaboration for the greater good of the land.

The program’s goal is simple but powerful: to equip young people with life-changing entrepreneurial and vocational skills, across both traditional crafts and digital-age professions.

Who It's For

Young people seeking practical skills

Equipping youth with real-world vocational training that leads to sustainable income and personal empowerment.

Women Pursuing Economic Freedom

Empowering women with marketable skills to achieve financial independence and contribute to community development.

Creatives and entrepreneurs

Supporting visionaries with tools to launch businesses, innovate, and express their talents commercially.

Job Seekers and Change Makers

Helping aspiring professionals gain relevant experience to thrive in today’s job market and drive impact.

The Skill Aquisition Program

The initiative is not a one-off; it's an unfolding legacy aligned with the Soun's strategic development plan to raise a skilled, self-sufficient population.

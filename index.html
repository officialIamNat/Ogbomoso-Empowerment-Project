This program offers six empowering vocational tracks designed to equip participants with hands-on, market-ready skills. From digital innovation to creative arts and essential services, each track opens doors to opportunity, income, and purpose. Explore the featured options below ‚Äî including the dynamic, multi-focus Information Technology (IT) track.

Information Technology Track

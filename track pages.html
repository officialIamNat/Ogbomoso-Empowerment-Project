Featured Tracks

Every track in this program is a gateway — to income, to purpose, and to a stronger Ogbomoso.

Web Design & Development

Learn to build responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Gain the skills to craft professional web solutions for businesses, brands, or personal portfolios in the digital space.

Baking & Pastries

Turn ingredients into income. Learn the techniques of making bread, cakes, snacks, and other baked goods — along with business tips to help you start or grow a successful bakery venture.

UI/UX Design

Master the art of creating seamless digital experiences. Understand design thinking, wireframing, and user-centered design that meets real-world needs and enhances usability across web and mobile platforms.

Household & Industrial Cleaning Production

Gain hands-on training in producing soaps, detergents, and disinfectants. This track teaches safe formulation and packaging for everyday and commercial cleaning needs — ready for immediate business or employment.

Data Analysis

Develop the ability to collect, interpret, and visualize data using tools like Excel and Power BI. Turn raw information into meaningful insights that drive smart decision-making in any industry.

Adire & Aso Oke Production

Learn the ancient craft of Yoruba textile making through dyeing and weaving techniques. This track blends tradition with modern appeal, empowering participants to create culturally rich and marketable fabrics.

Computer Networking

Build foundational knowledge in setting up, maintaining, and securing computer networks. Learn how the internet works behind the scenes and gain skills valued across IT support and cybersecurity fields.

Fashion Design

Discover how to bring creative clothing ideas to life. Learn fabric selection, cutting, sewing, and finishing — building the foundation for a fashion brand or professional tailoring service.

Event Planning & Decoration

Master the art of creating memorable events. From organizing logistics to designing beautiful spaces, this track teaches you how to deliver professional results for weddings, birthdays, and corporate functions.
